About TeachersFlow

It started with my mum. At 16, I watched her spend her evenings writing individual feedback for over a hundred students — not because she had to, but because she wanted to give each one something that would actually help them grow. I built TeachersFlow so teachers like her can keep giving everything to their students, without it costing everything they have.

TeachersFlow is an all-in-one AI app for teachers — handling grading, feedback, lesson planning, classroom management, and much more. When I started, I saw that most other apps were just ChatGPT wrappers. What I'm building is different: a living context library about every teacher and student, with a single AI agent that knows all of that context and can act inside the app — alongside specialized AI workspaces for specific jobs, from lesson planning down to simple tasks like adding a student to a group.
